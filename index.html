MISSION SAVE THE EARTH

Mission

Hello Captain!! Our Satellites have detected an anomaly. For some uknown resaon the gravity that was holding the astroid belt together has been lowered. The astroids are floating around our solar system.
Your mission should you choose to accept it is to take our BattelStar ship and destroy the asteroids headed to Earth

May the Developer's power be with you

How to Play

  • W:Thrust The BattelStar
  • A: Rotate The BattelStar Left
  • D: Rotate The BattelStar Right
  • Space: Shoot Laser
  • P: Pause Game
